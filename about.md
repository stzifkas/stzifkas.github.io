Hey, I'm **Sokratis** — a software engineer based in Thessaloniki, Greece.

I currently work at [Omilia](https://omilia.com), where I build things in the LLM/conversational AI space. Before that I spent a few years doing backend work (Python, Django, APIs, data pipelines) and a brief stint on computer vision and ML.

These days I'm mostly thinking about language models — how they work, where they break, and what you can actually do with them in production.

This blog is where I write about what I'm building, reading, or trying to understand. No set cadence, no niche — just stuff I find worth writing down.
